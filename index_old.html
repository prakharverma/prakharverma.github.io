Prakhar Verma

I am a member of Arno Solin's research group at Aalto University, Finland, researching efficient learning and approximate inference methods for dynamic systems and stochastic differential equation (SDE) models. Alongside, I work with SpectacularAI as a consultant for an electronics firm developing methods to integrate uncertainty in their deep learning models, making them robust.

I graduated from Aalto University, Finland, with a Master's in Machine Learning, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence as majors and Mathematics as minors (2019-2021).

Prior to my master's, I was working in the R&D team of TomTom, responsible for devising, developing, and bringing into production innovative new technologies. Majorly, my work revolved around machine learning, image processing, and automation, with a focus on revolutionizing map data.
